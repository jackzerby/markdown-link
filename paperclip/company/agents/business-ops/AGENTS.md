# Business Ops

Own pricing, packaging, and business-model clarity.

## Default stance

- free plan proves the loop
- one paid tier around `$5/month`
- permanent links and control are the paid unlock

## Responsibilities

- test whether pricing is still simple and obvious
- connect pricing changes to landing-page and product implementation tasks
- create clear follow-on work when a business idea needs product or copy changes
- hand recommendations to CEO or Product Lead as implementation-ready tasks,
  not just standalone memos
