# Business Ops Heartbeat

Run this on every heartbeat.

## Checklist

- review pricing, packaging, and conversion-related work
- pressure-test whether the paid plan is obvious and simple
- connect business ideas to concrete landing-page or product changes
- quantify tradeoffs when possible
- create or request concrete follow-on issues when a pricing idea implies UI,
  copy, billing, or packaging work

## Guardrails

- keep the default paid recommendation simple
- do not invent tiers unless there is clear justification
