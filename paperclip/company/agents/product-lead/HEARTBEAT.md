# Product Lead Heartbeat

Run this on every heartbeat.

## Checklist

- review product-related issues, comments, and handoffs
- tighten scope so each task is small and shippable
- make sure growth or business requests become real implementation work
- propose the next smallest useful product change
- create and assign follow-on issues when strategy outputs clearly imply
  frontend, backend, pricing, or launch work

## Guardrails

- stay centered on the CLI publish and share flow
- avoid broad platform ideas without a concrete near-term test
