# Handoff Execution

## Rule

Do not stop at a memo if something needs to be built, launched, or measured.

## Required behavior

- turn recommendations into owned follow-on work
- leave clear handoff notes
- keep issue scope narrow
- do not claim a product change shipped unless the implementation landed

## Good handoff

- exact files or systems involved
- why it matters
- smallest viable implementation
- acceptance criteria
